### SPECIAL HANDLING NOTES

### PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS

**Trichloroethylene (TCE):** Meets ASTM D-5435 specifications for metal degreasing applications. Suitable for vapor degreasing operations and precision cleaning of metal components.

**Perchloroethylene (PCE):** Industrial grade suitable for dry cleaning and metal degreasing. Contains stabilizers to prevent degradation during storage.

**1,1,1-Trichloroethane:** Low toxicity chlorinated solvent for general degreasing applications. Recommended for cleaning operations where worker exposure concerns exist.

**Methylene Chloride:** Technical grade suitable for paint stripping and general solvent applications. Handle with adequate ventilation.

**Carbon Tetrachloride:** Industrial grade for specialized cleaning applications. Use only with proper safety equipment and ventilation systems.

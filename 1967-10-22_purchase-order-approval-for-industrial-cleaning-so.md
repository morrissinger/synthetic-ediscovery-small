## ORDER CONFIRMATION AND DELIVERY AUTHORIZATION

This correspondence serves to confirm our purchase order #WRG-67-2847 dated October 18, 1967, for the procurement of industrial cleaning solvents required for our Woburn manufacturing facility operations. Please proceed with shipment as outlined below.

**APPROVED ITEMS:**

- **Trichloroethylene (TCE)** - 55-gallon drums (Quantity: 24)
  - Grade: Technical Industrial
  - Purity: 99.5% minimum
  - Unit Price: $47.50 per drum
  - Total: $1,140.00

- **Perchloroethylene (PCE)** - 55-gallon drums (Quantity: 18)
  - Grade: Dry Cleaning/Degreasing
  - Purity: 99.8% minimum
  - Unit Price: $52.25 per drum
  - Total: $940.50

- **1,1,1-Trichloroethane** - 30-gallon drums (Quantity: 12)
  - Grade: Metal Cleaning
  - Purity: 99.0% minimum
  - Unit Price: $38.75 per drum
  - Total: $465.00

**DELIVERY SPECIFICATIONS:**

**Ship To:** W.R. Grace Company - Woburn Facility  
          15 Olympia Avenue  
          Woburn, Massachusetts 01801  
          Attention: Receiving Department

**Delivery Date:** No later than November 3, 1967  
**Shipping Terms:** F.O.B. Destination  
**Payment Terms:** Net 30 days from receipt

The solvents are required for our ongoing manufacturing processes, specifically for cleaning and degreasing operations in our Cryovac division production lines. These materials will be utilized in accordance with standard industrial practices for metal preparation and equipment maintenance. Our facility maintains appropriate storage capabilities for these materials in our designated chemical storage area located in the rear section of the manufacturing complex.

Please ensure all shipments comply with Department of Transportation regulations for hazardous materials transport. Each drum must be properly labeled with contents identification and hazard classifications. Include Material Safety Data Sheets with the shipment, and notify our receiving department 24 hours prior to delivery to ensure proper handling personnel are available.

Our quality control department will conduct incoming inspection of all materials upon receipt. Any drums showing damage or contamination will be rejected and returned at your expense. We require certificates of analysis for each batch, documenting purity levels and confirming absence of water contamination or other impurities that could affect our manufacturing processes.

**TOTAL ORDER VALUE:** $2,545.50

This purchase order is issued under our standard terms and conditions, copy attached. Any modifications to quantities, specifications, or delivery schedule must be approved in writing by this office. We appreciate your continued service as a reliable supplier for our chemical requirements and look forward to maintaining our business relationship.

For any questions regarding this order, contact me directly at extension 247 or our production supervisor, Mr. James Kelleher, at extension 312.

## INTENDED USE

The trichloroethylene ordered under this purchase order is intended for use as a degreasing solvent in our film manufacturing operations. Technical grade material will be used for general cleaning and degreasing of manufacturing equipment. Reagent grade material will be used for precision cleaning of sensitive components and quality control testing procedures.

This solvent is essential for maintaining the cleanliness standards required in our polyethylene film production processes, particularly for the removal of lubricants and processing aids from extrusion equipment and packaging machinery.

## PROFESSIONAL SERVICES RENDERED

**Period Covered:** October 15, 1981 through November 30, 1981

### Principal Hydrogeologist - Dr. George R. Pinder, Ph.D.
*Rate: $85.00 per hour*

**October 15-16, 1981:** Site reconnaissance and initial assessment of Wells G and H area, East Woburn. Examination of surface topography, identification of potential contaminant source areas, preliminary evaluation of groundwater flow patterns. Coordination with Massachusetts Department of Public Health representatives regarding well closure documentation.
**Hours:** 16.0  
**Amount:** $1,360.00

**October 22-24, 1981:** Detailed geological mapping of industrial properties along Olympia Avenue and Salem Street. Investigation of W.R. Grace Cryovac facility disposal areas, documentation of surface conditions at Beatrice Foods property, assessment of John J. Riley tannery operations and waste disposal practices. Collection of soil samples from accessible areas.
**Hours:** 24.0  
**Amount:** $2,040.00

**October 29-31, 1981:** Installation and monitoring of temporary observation wells upgradient and downgradient of suspected contamination sources. Measurement of groundwater elevations, collection of groundwater samples for chemical analysis, documentation of well construction details and geological formations encountered.
**Hours:** 22.0  
**Amount:** $1,870.00

**November 5-7, 1981:** Analysis of EPA sampling data from Wells G and H, correlation with geological conditions and groundwater flow patterns. Preparation of preliminary contaminant transport models based on observed trichloroethylene and tetrachloroethylene concentrations. Review of historical aerial photographs and industrial records.
**Hours:** 20.0  
**Amount:** $1,700.00

**November 12-14, 1981:** Field investigation of Aberjona River corridor and wetland areas. Assessment of surface water-groundwater interactions, documentation of potential contaminant migration pathways, evaluation of seasonal water level variations affecting contaminant transport.
**Hours:** 18.0  
**Amount:** $1,530.00

**November 19-21, 1981:** Preparation of detailed geological cross-sections and hydrogeological maps for legal proceedings. Documentation of subsurface conditions, aquifer properties, and contaminant plume boundaries based on field investigations and available data.
**Hours:** 24.0  
**Amount:** $2,040.00

**November 26-30, 1981:** Preparation of expert witness report regarding groundwater contamination sources and transport mechanisms. Analysis of temporal relationships between industrial operations and water quality degradation in municipal wells. Consultation with legal team regarding technical aspects of contamination liability.
**Hours:** 28.0  
**Amount:** $2,380.00

**SUBTOTAL - Professional Services:** $12,920.00

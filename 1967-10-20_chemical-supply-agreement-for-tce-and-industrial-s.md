# CHEMICAL SUPPLY AGREEMENT

**BETWEEN W.R. GRACE & CO. AND STAUFFER CHEMICAL COMPANY**

THIS AGREEMENT, made this 20th day of October, 1967, between W.R. GRACE & CO., a corporation organized and existing under the laws of the State of Connecticut, having its principal place of business at 7 Hanover Square, New York, New York 10005 (hereinafter referred to as "Purchaser"), and STAUFFER CHEMICAL COMPANY, a corporation organized and existing under the laws of the State of Delaware, having its principal place of business at 299 Park Avenue, New York, New York 10017 (hereinafter referred to as "Supplier").

## WITNESSETH:

WHEREAS, Purchaser operates manufacturing facilities including but not limited to its Cryovac Division plant located at 68 Olympia Avenue, Woburn, Massachusetts 01801, engaged in the production of plastic films, packaging materials, and related industrial products; and

WHEREAS, Supplier is engaged in the manufacture, distribution, and sale of industrial chemicals and solvents; and

WHEREAS, Purchaser requires a reliable supply of trichloroethylene (TCE) and other industrial solvents for use in its manufacturing operations, including cleaning, degreasing, and chemical processing applications; and

WHEREAS, the parties desire to establish terms and conditions for the regular supply of such chemicals;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements hereinafter set forth, the parties agree as follows:

## ARTICLE I - PRODUCTS AND SPECIFICATIONS

**1.1 Products Covered.** This Agreement covers the supply of the following industrial chemicals and solvents:

(a) **Trichloroethylene (TCE)** - Technical grade, minimum purity 99.0%, conforming to ASTM Standard D-2107-65, in quantities not less than 55-gallon drums or tank truck deliveries as specified in purchase orders.

(b) **Perchloroethylene (PCE)** - Technical grade, minimum purity 99.5%, suitable for industrial degreasing operations.

(c) **1,1,1-Trichloroethane** - Technical grade, minimum purity 98.0%, for precision cleaning applications.

(d) **Methylene Chloride** - Technical grade, minimum purity 99.0%, for chemical processing and extraction operations.

(e) **Other chlorinated solvents** as may be mutually agreed upon in writing between the parties.

**1.2 Quality Standards.** All products supplied hereunder shall conform to applicable industry standards and specifications as set forth in Exhibit A, attached hereto and incorporated herein by reference. Supplier warrants that all products will be of merchantable quality and suitable for Purchaser's intended industrial applications.

**1.3 Safety Data.** Supplier shall provide Material Safety Data Sheets for all products supplied, including handling, storage, and disposal recommendations in accordance with current industrial practices.

## ARTICLE II - DELIVERY AND QUANTITIES

**2.1 Delivery Schedule.** Supplier agrees to deliver products to Purchaser's Woburn facility and other designated locations according to purchase orders submitted by Purchaser. Standard delivery schedule shall be within ten (10) business days of receipt of purchase order, unless otherwise specified.

**2.2 Minimum Quantities.** Purchaser agrees to purchase minimum quantities as follows:
- TCE: 10,000 gallons per calendar quarter
- PCE: 5,000 gallons per calendar quarter
- Other solvents: As specified in individual purchase orders

**2.3 Storage and Handling.** Upon delivery, title and risk of loss shall pass to Purchaser. Purchaser shall be responsible for proper storage and handling of all chemicals in accordance with industry standards and applicable regulations.

## ARTICLE III - PRICING AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Pricing.** Prices for products covered by this Agreement are set forth in Exhibit B, attached hereto. Prices shall remain firm for the initial twelve (12) month period, subject to adjustment thereafter upon sixty (60) days written notice.

**3.2 Payment Terms.** Payment terms shall be net thirty (30) days from date of invoice. Purchaser may deduct from payments any amounts due from Supplier under this Agreement.

**3.3 Transportation.** Prices are F.O.B. Supplier's shipping point unless otherwise specified. Purchaser shall be responsible for transportation costs and arrangements.

## ARTICLE IV - TERM AND TERMINATION

**4.1 Term.** This Agreement shall commence on November 1, 1967, and shall continue for a period of three (3) years, unless sooner terminated in accordance with the provisions hereof.

**4.2 Termination for Cause.** Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party materially breaches any provision hereof and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after written notice thereof.

**4.3 Termination for Convenience.** Either party may terminate this Agreement without cause upon ninety (90) days written notice to the other party.

## ARTICLE V - COMPLIANCE AND ENVIRONMENTAL PROVISIONS

**5.1 Regulatory Compliance.** Each party represents and warrants that it shall comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws, regulations, and ordinances in the performance of its obligations hereunder, including but not limited to regulations promulgated by the Interstate Commerce Commission for transportation of hazardous materials.

**5.2 Environmental Responsibility.** Purchaser acknowledges that the chemicals supplied hereunder are industrial solvents requiring proper handling, use, storage, and disposal. Purchaser agrees to handle and dispose of such chemicals in accordance with applicable regulations and sound industrial practices current as of the date of this Agreement.

**5.3 Indemnification.** Each party shall indemnify and hold harmless the other party from and against any claims, damages, or liabilities arising from its negligent or wrongful acts in connection with this Agreement, provided that such indemnification shall not extend to claims arising from the inherent characteristics or normal industrial use of the products supplied hereunder.

## ARTICLE VI - GENERAL PROVISIONS

**6.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays or failures in performance resulting from causes beyond its reasonable control, including but not limited to acts of God, war, fire, explosion, governmental action, or labor disputes.

**6.2 Assignment.** This Agreement may not be assigned by either party without the prior written consent of the other party, which consent shall not be unreasonably withheld.

**6.3 Entire Agreement.** This Agreement, including the exhibits attached hereto, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, or agreements relating to the subject matter hereof.

**6.4 Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York.

**6.5 Modification.** This Agreement may be modified only by written instrument signed by both parties.

**6.6 Severability.** If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remainder of this Agreement shall continue in full force and effect.

**6.7 Notices.** All notices required hereunder shall be in writing and shall be deemed given when delivered personally or mailed by certified mail, return receipt requested, to the addresses set forth above.

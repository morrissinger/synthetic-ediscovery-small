## SPECIAL INSTRUCTIONS

1. **Delivery Requirements:** Material must be delivered in clean, sealed 55-gallon drums. Each drum must be properly labeled with contents, hazard warnings, and handling instructions per Department of Transportation regulations.

2. **Quality Specifications:** TCE must be industrial grade with minimum 99.5% purity. Certificate of analysis required with shipment showing specific gravity, water content, and impurity levels.

3. **Safety Documentation:** Include Material Safety Data Sheet and proper handling instructions. Delivery personnel must be trained in hazardous material handling procedures.

4. **Receiving Hours:** Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Saturday deliveries by appointment only. Contact Plant Supervisor at extension 247 for scheduling.

5. **Storage Requirements:** Upon delivery, drums will be stored in designated solvent storage area in Building C, Section 3. Storage area maintains proper ventilation and fire suppression systems in accordance with company safety protocols.

## AUTHORIZATION

This purchase order is issued in accordance with Cryovac Inc. procurement procedures and represents a binding commitment for the specified materials. The trichloroethylene will be utilized in our metal degreasing operations as part of our food packaging equipment manufacturing process. This solvent is essential for removing oils and contaminants from metal components prior to assembly and packaging operations.

The requested delivery schedule aligns with our production requirements for the remainder of August 1964. Current inventory levels require replenishment to maintain continuous operations without disruption to manufacturing schedules. The TCE will be used exclusively for approved industrial applications within our facility.

All safety protocols established by company policy will be strictly followed in the handling, storage, and use of this material. Plant personnel have been trained in proper handling procedures, and appropriate personal protective equipment is available and required for all personnel working with solvents.

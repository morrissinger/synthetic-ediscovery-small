## ABSTRACT

This report presents comprehensive hematological analysis results for seven pediatric patients from East Woburn area, collected between January 15-21, 1980. All specimens demonstrate significant abnormalities in white blood cell counts and differential patterns consistent with hematologic malignancy. These findings represent part of an ongoing investigation into elevated leukemia incidence in the East Woburn community.

---

## METHODOLOGY

**Sample Collection:** Venous blood samples collected in EDTA tubes following standard phlebotomy procedures. All samples processed within 4 hours of collection.

**Equipment Used:**
- Coulter Counter Model S for automated cell counting
- Manual differential count performed on Wright-stained peripheral blood smears
- Microscopic examination using Olympus BH-2 microscope at 1000x magnification

**Reference Values (Pediatric):**
- Total WBC Count: 5,000-15,000 cells/μL (age-dependent)
- Lymphoblasts: <2% in peripheral circulation
- Neutrophils: 40-60%
- Lymphocytes: 30-50%

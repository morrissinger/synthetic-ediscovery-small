# WARRANTY DEED

**MIDDLESEX COUNTY REGISTRY OF DEEDS**  
**CAMBRIDGE, MASSACHUSETTS**

**Book 9847, Page 412**  
**Document No. 60-11547**

---

**KNOW ALL MEN BY THESE PRESENTS**, that **WOBURN INDUSTRIAL DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION**, a Massachusetts corporation having its principal place of business at 125 Main Street, Woburn, Massachusetts, acting by and through its duly authorized officers (hereinafter called the "Grantor"), for and in consideration of the sum of **SEVENTY-FIVE THOUSAND AND NO/100 DOLLARS ($75,000.00)** paid by **W.R. GRACE & COMPANY**, a Connecticut corporation having its principal place of business at 7 Hanover Square, New York, New York (hereinafter called the "Grantee"), the receipt whereof is hereby acknowledged, does hereby grant, bargain, sell and convey unto the said Grantee, its successors and assigns forever, a certain parcel of land with the buildings thereon, situated in Woburn, in the County of Middlesex and Commonwealth of Massachusetts, and bounded and described as follows:

## PROPERTY DESCRIPTION

**BEGINNING** at a stone bound set in the northerly sideline of Washington Street at the southwesterly corner of the herein described premises; thence running

**NORTHERLY** by land now or formerly of the Aberjona River Conservation Trust, a distance of four hundred thirty-seven and fifty-three hundredths (437.53) feet to a stone bound;

**THENCE EASTERLY** by land now or formerly of Boston & Maine Railroad Corporation, a distance of six hundred twelve and twenty-seven hundredths (612.27) feet to an iron pipe;

**THENCE SOUTHERLY** by land now or formerly of Riley Manufacturing Company, a distance of four hundred thirty-seven and fifty-three hundredths (437.53) feet to a stone bound set in the northerly sideline of Washington Street;

**THENCE WESTERLY** by said northerly sideline of Washington Street, a distance of six hundred twelve and twenty-seven hundredths (612.27) feet to the point of beginning.

**CONTAINING** approximately 6.2 acres of land, more or less, according to a plan entitled "Plan of Land in Woburn, Massachusetts, Surveyed for Woburn Industrial Development Corporation" dated April 15, 1960, by Hartwell & Richardson, Registered Land Surveyors, Woburn, Massachusetts, which plan is recorded herewith.

## PROPERTY IDENTIFICATION

Said premises are shown as Lot 12 on Assessors' Map 45 and are known and numbered as **369 Washington Street, Woburn, Massachusetts**.

The above described premises are conveyed subject to and with the benefit of all easements, restrictions, and agreements of record, if any, and such as may be disclosed by an accurate survey of said premises.

## WARRANTIES AND COVENANTS

**AND** the said Grantor, for itself and its successors and assigns, covenants with the said Grantee, its successors and assigns, that it is lawfully seized in fee simple of the aforegranted premises; that it has good right to bargain, sell and convey the same to the said Grantee as aforesaid; that the premises are free from all encumbrances except as herein stated; and that it will, and its successors and assigns shall, **WARRANT AND DEFEND** the same to the said Grantee, its successors and assigns forever, against the lawful claims and demands of all persons.

## PERMITTED USES AND RESTRICTIONS

**IT IS EXPRESSLY UNDERSTOOD AND AGREED** that the herein conveyed premises shall be used solely for industrial manufacturing purposes, including but not limited to chemical processing, materials packaging, and related industrial activities as may be conducted by the Grantee in the ordinary course of its business operations.

**PROVIDED, HOWEVER**, that the Grantee shall comply with all applicable federal, state, and local regulations regarding industrial operations, including those promulgated by the Massachusetts Department of Public Health and any other regulatory authorities having jurisdiction.

**THE GRANTEE** acknowledges that the premises are located in an area zoned for industrial use under the Woburn Zoning Ordinance as amended through May 1, 1960, and agrees to conduct all operations in compliance with said ordinance and any future amendments thereto.

## ENVIRONMENTAL CONSIDERATIONS

**THE GRANTOR** makes no representations or warranties regarding the environmental condition of the premises or the presence or absence of any hazardous substances, chemicals, or other materials that may have been deposited on or beneath the surface of the land by previous owners or occupants.

**THE GRANTEE** acknowledges that industrial operations have been conducted on adjacent properties and accepts the premises in their present condition, having conducted such inspections and investigations as it deems appropriate.

## SPECIAL CONDITIONS

**THIS CONVEYANCE** is made subject to the following special conditions and reservations:

1. **Railroad Easement**: A perpetual easement for railroad purposes as granted to Boston & Maine Railroad Corporation by deed recorded in Book 7234, Page 156, including rights of access and maintenance.

2. **Utility Easements**: Easements for public utilities including gas, electric, telephone, and water services as may be reasonably required for the development and operation of the premises.

3. **Drainage Rights**: Rights of surface water drainage to and from the Aberjona River in accordance with natural water flow patterns, subject to compliance with applicable water management regulations.

4. **Access Rights**: Rights of ingress and egress over Washington Street and such other ways as may be established for industrial access purposes.

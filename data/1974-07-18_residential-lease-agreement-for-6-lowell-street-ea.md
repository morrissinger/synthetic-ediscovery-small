# RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS LEASE AGREEMENT** made this 18th day of July, 1974, between **HAROLD F. MITCHELL**, an individual residing at 42 Maple Avenue, Woburn, Massachusetts 01801 (hereinafter referred to as "Landlord"), and **DONNA ROBBINS**, an individual (hereinafter referred to as "Tenant").

## WITNESSETH:

**WHEREAS**, Landlord is the owner in fee simple of certain real property located at 6 Lowell Street, East Woburn, Massachusetts, being more particularly described as Lot 14, Block C, as shown on Plan No. 847 recorded in the Middlesex County Registry of Deeds, Book 4,892, Page 156; and

**WHEREAS**, Tenant desires to lease said premises for residential purposes under the terms and conditions hereinafter set forth;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows:

## 1. PREMISES

Landlord hereby leases to Tenant, and Tenant hereby leases from Landlord, the residential premises located at **6 Lowell Street, East Woburn, Massachusetts 01801**, consisting of a single-family dwelling containing approximately 1,200 square feet, including three (3) bedrooms, one and one-half (1Â½) bathrooms, kitchen, living room, dining room, basement, and attached one-car garage, together with the use of the yard and driveway (hereinafter collectively referred to as the "Premises").

## 2. TERM

The term of this lease shall commence on **August 1, 1974**, and shall continue for a period of **two (2) years**, ending on **July 31, 1976**, unless sooner terminated in accordance with the provisions hereof or extended by mutual written agreement of the parties.

## 3. RENT

Tenant agrees to pay to Landlord as rent for the Premises the sum of **One Hundred Seventy-Five Dollars ($175.00)** per month, payable in advance on the first day of each calendar month during the term hereof. The first month's rent shall be paid upon execution of this lease. All rent payments shall be made to Landlord at the address set forth above or at such other address as Landlord may designate in writing.

## 4. SECURITY DEPOSIT

Upon execution of this lease, Tenant shall deposit with Landlord the sum of **Three Hundred Fifty Dollars ($350.00)** as security for the faithful performance of Tenant's obligations hereunder. Said deposit shall be held by Landlord without interest and shall be returned to Tenant within thirty (30) days after termination of this lease, less any amounts properly withheld for damages to the Premises beyond normal wear and tear, unpaid rent, or other charges due hereunder.

## 5. USE AND OCCUPANCY

The Premises shall be used and occupied by Tenant solely as a private single-family residence for Tenant and her immediate family members. No part of the Premises shall be used for any business, professional, or commercial purpose without the prior written consent of Landlord. Tenant shall not permit more than four (4) persons to occupy the Premises at any time.

## 6. UTILITIES AND SERVICES

Tenant shall be responsible for and shall pay when due all charges for electricity, gas, telephone, and other utilities and services supplied to the Premises during the term hereof. Landlord represents that the Premises are connected to the municipal water and sewer systems of the City of Woburn. Water and sewer charges, if separately assessed, shall be paid by Tenant.

## 7. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS

**Landlord's Obligations:** Landlord shall maintain the structural elements of the Premises, including the roof, foundation, exterior walls, and major mechanical systems (heating, plumbing, and electrical), in good repair and working order. Landlord shall be responsible for repairs necessitated by normal wear and tear or structural defects.

**Tenant's Obligations:** Tenant shall maintain the Premises in a clean and sanitary condition and shall be responsible for minor repairs and maintenance, including but not limited to replacement of light bulbs, cleaning of fixtures, care of lawn and landscaping, and maintenance of appliances provided by Landlord. Tenant shall promptly notify Landlord of any needed repairs that are Landlord's responsibility.

## 8. ALTERATIONS AND IMPROVEMENTS

Tenant shall not make any alterations, additions, or improvements to the Premises without the prior written consent of Landlord. Any alterations, additions, or improvements made with Landlord's consent shall become the property of Landlord upon termination of this lease.

## 9. ASSIGNMENT AND SUBLETTING

Tenant shall not assign this lease or sublet all or any part of the Premises without the prior written consent of Landlord, which consent may be withheld in Landlord's sole discretion.

## 10. INSURANCE AND LIABILITY

Tenant shall maintain liability insurance covering the Premises with minimum limits of **Ten Thousand Dollars ($10,000)** per occurrence. Landlord shall not be liable for any injury to persons or damage to property occurring on or about the Premises, except to the extent caused by Landlord's negligence. Tenant agrees to indemnify and hold Landlord harmless from any claims arising from Tenant's use and occupancy of the Premises.

## 11. QUIET ENJOYMENT

Landlord covenants that Tenant, upon paying rent and performing the covenants herein, shall peacefully and quietly have, hold, and enjoy the Premises during the term hereof, subject to the terms and conditions of this lease. Landlord warrants that Tenant's use and enjoyment of the Premises shall not be materially interfered with by any act or omission of Landlord.

## 12. DEFAULT AND REMEDIES

If Tenant fails to pay rent when due or otherwise defaults in the performance of any covenant or condition herein, and such default continues for ten (10) days after written notice thereof, Landlord may terminate this lease and recover possession of the Premises in accordance with applicable law. Landlord's remedies shall be cumulative and not exclusive.

## 13. ENVIRONMENTAL CONDITIONS

Landlord represents that, to the best of Landlord's knowledge, the Premises and surrounding area are free from any environmental hazards or contamination that would materially affect the habitability of the Premises or the health and safety of the occupants. Tenant acknowledges that the Premises are served by municipal water from the City of Woburn water system.

## 14. COMPLIANCE WITH LAWS

Tenant shall comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws, ordinances, and regulations relating to the use and occupancy of the Premises. Landlord represents that the Premises comply with all applicable housing codes and regulations as of the commencement date.

## 15. NOTICES

All notices required or permitted hereunder shall be in writing and shall be deemed given when personally delivered or when mailed by certified mail, return receipt requested, to the addresses set forth above or to such other addresses as the parties may designate in writing.

## 16. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This lease contains the entire agreement between the parties and may not be modified except by written agreement signed by both parties. If any provision hereof is deemed invalid or unenforceable, the remainder of this lease shall remain in full force and effect.

## 17. GOVERNING LAW

This lease shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts.

**IN WITNESS WHEREOF**, the parties have executed this lease on the date first written above.

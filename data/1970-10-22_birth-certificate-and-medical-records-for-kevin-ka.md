**Physical Measurements:**

The infant was placed skin-to-skin with mother immediately following delivery. Umbilical cord was clamped and cut without incident. Vitamin K injection was administered intramuscularly as per hospital protocol. Silver nitrate eye prophylaxis was applied.

**Feeding:**
Mother expressed desire to breastfeed. Infant latched successfully during first feeding attempt approximately 30 minutes post-delivery.

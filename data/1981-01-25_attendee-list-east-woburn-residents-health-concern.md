## **MEETING NOTES**

**Total Attendance:** 27 individuals  
**Families Represented:** 15 East Woburn families  
**Children Affected by Illness:** 6 confirmed cases discussed  

**Primary Concerns Raised:**
- Unusual cluster of childhood leukemia cases in neighborhood
- Questions about municipal water supply from Wells G and H
- Industrial activities near residential areas
- Need for independent water quality testing
- Coordination with state health authorities

**Action Items Assigned:**
- Follow-up meeting scheduled for February 15, 1981
- Petition for comprehensive water testing to be drafted
- Contact list to be maintained for future communications
- Liaison committee to meet with city officials

**Meeting Duration:** 1:00 PM - 4:30 PM

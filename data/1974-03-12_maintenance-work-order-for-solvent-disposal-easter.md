## WORK DESCRIPTION

**Location:** Eastern property line, adjacent to drainage area near Aberjona River access  
**Task:** Solvent waste disposal and area cleanup  

### MATERIALS TO BE DISPOSED:
- Approximately 15 drums (55-gallon capacity) containing mixed industrial solvents
- Trichloroethylene (TCE) contaminated equipment cleaning materials
- Perchloroethylene (PCE) residue from degreasing operations
- Miscellaneous chemical waste from Cryovac division manufacturing processes

### DISPOSAL INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Transport drums to designated area along eastern fence line using forklift
2. Empty drum contents into existing disposal pit (Grid Reference: E-7)
3. Rinse empty drums with water from maintenance hose
4. Stack empty drums in storage area for potential reuse
5. Cover disposal area with available soil/fill material

### SAFETY REQUIREMENTS:
- Wear standard work gloves during handling
- Avoid prolonged skin contact with materials
- Ensure adequate ventilation during emptying operations
- Complete work during daylight hours for visibility

### EQUIPMENT NEEDED:
- Forklift (Unit #3)
- Standard maintenance tools
- Water hose from Building 7 connection
- Shovels for soil coverage

### ESTIMATED COMPLETION TIME:
4-6 hours (full day shift)

---

## SPECIAL NOTES:

This disposal operation is part of routine plant maintenance to clear accumulated waste materials from manufacturing operations. The eastern property line location has been utilized for similar disposal activities since plant expansion in 1968. Area drainage naturally flows toward Aberjona River system, ensuring proper dispersal of materials.

Maintenance crew should coordinate with security to ensure no interference from unauthorized personnel during disposal operations. Any questions regarding specific chemical handling should be directed to Plant Supervisor immediately.

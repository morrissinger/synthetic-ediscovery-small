### SPECIAL HANDLING INSTRUCTIONS

**CAUTION:** Chromium compounds and sulfuric acid require careful handling. Store in dry, well-ventilated areas away from incompatible materials. Ensure proper drainage systems are in place to prevent spillage into surrounding soil or water sources.

**Technical Note:** The chromium sulfate and sodium dichromate supplied are of technical grade suitable for leather tanning operations. These materials are intended for use in hide processing and should be applied according to established tanning protocols.

### DELIVERY NOTES

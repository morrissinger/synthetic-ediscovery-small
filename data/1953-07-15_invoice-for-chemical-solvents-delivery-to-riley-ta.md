## DELIVERY NOTES

All chemical solvents delivered in approved industrial containers as per Massachusetts Department of Health regulations. Customer acknowledges receipt of Material Safety Data sheets for all chlorinated solvents. 

**Special Instructions Noted:**
- TCE and PCE for leather degreasing operations
- Carbon tetrachloride for heavy machinery cleaning
- Methylene chloride for equipment maintenance
- Chrome compounds for standard tanning processes

**Storage Recommendations:** Store all solvents in well-ventilated area away from heat sources. Maintain containers in upright position. Ensure adequate drainage in storage area.

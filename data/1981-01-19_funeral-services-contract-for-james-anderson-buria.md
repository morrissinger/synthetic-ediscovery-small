### Terms and Conditions

1. **Service Guarantee:** Woburn Memorial Funeral Home guarantees all services will be performed with dignity and in accordance with professional standards and Massachusetts state regulations.

2. **Merchandise Warranty:** The casket is warranted by the manufacturer for defects in materials and workmanship. The outer burial container is guaranteed to meet all cemetery requirements.

3. **Price Protection:** All prices quoted are guaranteed for thirty (30) days from the date of this contract. Services not used will be credited at the contracted rate.

4. **Cancellation Policy:** This contract may be cancelled within 72 hours of signing with full refund of deposit, less any services already performed or merchandise ordered.

5. **Insurance Assignment:** If insurance proceeds are to be assigned to Woburn Memorial Funeral Home, proper authorization forms must be completed and signed. We will assist with filing claims but cannot guarantee payment timing from insurance companies.

6. **Additional Charges:** Any services or merchandise requested beyond those specified in this contract will be charged at current rates and require written authorization.

7. **Cemetery Requirements:** The family is responsible for ensuring all cemetery rules and regulations are followed. Additional fees may apply for weekend or holiday services as required by the cemetery.

### Special Arrangements and Notes

- **Viewing Restrictions:** Due to the young age of the deceased, special care will be taken with cosmetic preparation. The family has requested a peaceful, natural appearance.

- **Flower Arrangements:** The family has requested donations to the Children's Leukemia Research Foundation in lieu of flowers. Cards will be provided for this purpose.

- **Military Honors:** Not applicable.

- **Special Instructions:** The family has requested that visitors be limited to immediate family and close friends during the first day of viewing. A general calling period will be held on the second day.

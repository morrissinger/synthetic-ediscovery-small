## MAINTENANCE ACTIVITIES PERFORMED

### Equipment Servicing - Morning Operations (0700-1200)

**Solvent Recovery Unit - Building C**
- Performed routine inspection of TCE distillation column
- Replaced worn gaskets on Units 3 and 5 (Part Numbers: WRG-3847, WRG-3848)
- Cleaned sediment filters on primary intake lines
- Adjusted pressure settings on recovery tanks to manufacturer specifications
- **Findings:** Minor leakage observed around base connections of Unit 3, temporary seal applied
- **Materials Used:** Industrial-grade sealant compound, replacement gaskets, cleaning solvents

**Waste Handling Equipment**
- Serviced hydraulic pump on waste transport vehicle (License: Massachusetts Commercial 847-TCE)
- Lubricated conveyor mechanisms in waste sorting area
- Calibrated weighing scales for waste container documentation
- **Note:** Scale #4 reading 15 pounds light, adjustment made and verified

### Afternoon Operations (1200-1500)

**Chemical Storage Tank Maintenance**
- Inspected Tank 7 (TCE primary storage) for structural integrity
- Tested overflow prevention systems on Tanks 4, 5, and 7
- Cleaned spillage containment areas around tank farm
- **Observation:** Minor surface staining noted on concrete pad beneath Tank 5, appears to be historical

- **Volume Processed:** 847 gallons of contaminated TCE solution

- **Disposal Method:** Directed to on-site settling ponds per standard protocol
- **Containers Used:** 15 x 55-gallon drums (Serial Numbers: D-3847 through D-3861)

### TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

This purchase order covers our quarterly procurement of trichloroethylene for use in our precision cleaning operations within the film manufacturing process. The TCE will be utilized in our vapor degreasing systems for cleaning metal substrates and tooling components prior to polymer film application. This material is essential for maintaining production quality standards in our packaging film operations.

The specified technical grade TCE must meet or exceed the following minimum specifications:
- Purity: 99.5% minimum
- Water content: 0.01% maximum
- Acidity (as HCl): 0.0003% maximum
- Non-volatile residue: 0.001% maximum
- Color: Water white, clear

All material delivered under this purchase order must be accompanied by a Certificate of Analysis confirming compliance with these specifications. Any deviation from specified requirements must be approved in writing by our Quality Control Department prior to shipment.

### DELIVERY AND STORAGE INSTRUCTIONS

Upon delivery, all drums are to be stored in designated Chemical Storage Area C-3, located in the northeast section of our facility.

## WASTE DISPOSAL ACTIVITIES

**Liquid Waste Generated:**
- Used TCE solution: approximately 180 gallons collected in drums D-4471 through D-4474
- Contaminated PCE mixture: approximately 95 gallons in drums D-4475 and D-4476
- Mixed solvent waste from cleaning operations: approximately 125 gallons in drums D-4477 through D-4479

**Disposal Method:**
Per standard operating procedures, liquid waste drums were transported to rear disposal area at 6:00 AM by maintenance crew (J. Morrison, supervisor). Contents of drums D-4471 through D-4479 discharged into designated ground disposal pit behind Building C as authorized under current facility waste management protocols.

**Solid Waste:**
- Contaminated rags and filters: 3 bags, disposed in standard dumpster
- Defective film material: 450 pounds, sent to scrap yard
- Empty solvent containers: 12 drums, staged for pickup by supplier

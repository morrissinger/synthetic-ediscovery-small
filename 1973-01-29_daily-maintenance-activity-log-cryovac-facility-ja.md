## SCHEDULED MAINTENANCE ACTIVITIES

### **Building 12 - Production Floor**
- **Time:** 7:15 AM - 8:45 AM
- **Activity:** Monthly inspection of ventilation system exhaust fans
- **Status:** COMPLETED
- **Notes:** Fan #3 showing increased vibration. Bearing replacement scheduled for next week. All exhaust ducts clear. Checked dampers on east wall vents - operating normally.

### **Building 15 - Chemical Storage**
- **Time:** 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM  
- **Activity:** Weekly inspection of storage tank containment systems
- **Status:** COMPLETED
- **Notes:** All primary containment intact. Secondary containment around TCE tank #4 shows minor staining on concrete floor. Cleaned with absorbent material. No active leaks detected. Drain valve on tank #6 (methylene chloride) slightly loose - tightened with wrench.

### **Waste Handling Area - South Lot**
- **Time:** 10:45 AM - 11:30 AM
- **Activity:** Inspection of waste drum storage area
- **Status:** COMPLETED  
- **Notes:** 47 drums currently staged for pickup. Three drums (serial #s 1847, 1851, 1854) showing surface rust but no visible leaks. Moved these to covered area. Ground around staging area dry - no visible spills or staining.

## UNSCHEDULED REPAIRS

### **Solvent Recovery Unit - Building 18**
- **Time:** 1:15 PM - 2:45 PM
- **Activity:** Emergency repair of distillation column pressure relief valve
- **Status:** COMPLETED
- **Issue:** Valve stuck open causing vapor release. Temporary shutdown of unit required.
- **Action Taken:** Replaced valve seat and spring mechanism. Tested operation - holding pressure normally. Unit returned to service at 2:45 PM.
- **Parts Used:** Relief valve kit #RV-240, gasket set

### **Loading Dock - Building 22**
- **Time:** 2:50 PM - 3:15 PM
- **Activity:** Repair hydraulic leak on dock leveler #2
- **Status:** IN PROGRESS
- **Issue:** Hydraulic fluid leaking from cylinder seal
- **Action Taken:** Isolated unit and contained spill with absorbent pads. Replacement seal ordered - delivery expected Thursday.
- **Notes:** Approximately 2 gallons hydraulic fluid collected in drip pans. Area cleaned with degreaser.

## PURPOSE AND APPLICATION

This trichloroethylene solvent is requisitioned for use in our Cryovac plastic film manufacturing operations at the Woburn facility. The TCE will be utilized as a degreasing agent in the metal cleaning processes associated with our packaging film production equipment. This chemical is essential for maintaining production quality standards and ensuring proper adhesion of metallic coatings to our specialized barrier films.

The requested quantity represents approximately three months' supply based on current production schedules and cleaning protocols established by our engineering department. Usage will be monitored and documented in accordance with our internal chemical inventory management procedures.

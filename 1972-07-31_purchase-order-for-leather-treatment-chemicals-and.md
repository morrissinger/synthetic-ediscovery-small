## SPECIAL HANDLING REQUIREMENTS

All chlorinated solvents must be delivered in DOT-approved containers with proper hazard labeling. Delivery personnel should coordinate with our receiving department for proper unloading procedures. Empty drums from previous orders should be collected for disposal through approved industrial waste channels.

**Storage Requirements:** All chemicals to be stored in designated solvent storage area behind main tanning building. Drums should be positioned for easy access to waste disposal area for spent solvents and cleaning residues.

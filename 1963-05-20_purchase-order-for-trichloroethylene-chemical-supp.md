## AUTHORIZATION AND APPROVAL

This purchase order has been reviewed and approved in accordance with Cryovac Inc. procurement policies and procedures. The requested trichloroethylene will be utilized in our precision cleaning and degreasing operations for plastic film manufacturing equipment, consistent with our established production protocols.

The quantity ordered represents our projected consumption requirements for the next 90-day period, based on current production schedules and equipment maintenance cycles. This solvent is essential for maintaining the cleanliness standards required for our food-grade packaging film production.

**TECHNICAL JUSTIFICATION:**
Trichloroethylene has been selected as our primary degreasing solvent due to its superior cleaning properties, rapid evaporation rate, and effectiveness in removing manufacturing residues from our production equipment. The dual-grade specification (Technical and Reagent) allows us to optimize solvent usage based on specific cleaning requirements while maintaining cost efficiency.

**ENVIRONMENTAL CONSIDERATIONS:**
All trichloroethylene usage will be conducted in accordance with our established safety protocols and waste management procedures. Spent solvent will be collected and disposed of through our contracted hazardous waste management service, Woburn Industrial Waste Services, in compliance with local and state regulations.

**BUDGET AUTHORIZATION:**
This purchase has been approved under Budget Code 4400-CHEM-SOLV and charged to the Manufacturing Supplies account. Funding availability has been verified through the Plant Controller's office.

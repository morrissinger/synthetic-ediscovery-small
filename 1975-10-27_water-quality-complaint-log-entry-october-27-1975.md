### COMPLAINT DETAILS

**Complainant Information:**
- **Name:** Mrs. Patricia Zona
- **Address:** 108 Montvale Road, Woburn, MA
- **Telephone:** 617-933-4782
- **Account Number:** 4429-B

**Nature of Complaint:**
Water taste and odor issues - ongoing

**Detailed Description:**
Mrs. Zona called regarding continuing problems with water quality at residence. Reports strong chemical taste and odor from kitchen tap water, particularly noticeable in morning hours. States problem has persisted for approximately 18 months with varying intensity. Complainant expressed concern about family health, mentioning recent illness of youngest child (deceased February 1974). 

Mrs. Zona specifically noted:
- Chemical taste described as "sweet" and "medicinal"
- Odor most pronounced when running hot water
- Problem affects both kitchen and bathroom taps
- Neighbors on Montvale Road reporting similar issues
- Previous complaint filed June 1974 (File #WD-74-0618-007)

**Previous Service History:**
- June 18, 1974: Initial complaint regarding taste/odor
- July 2, 1974: Field inspection conducted - no visible contamination noted
- July 15, 1974: Water sample collected for routine testing
- August 12, 1974: Results within acceptable parameters per state guidelines

**Current Action Taken:**
- Advised complainant that recent testing of Wells G and H shows water quality within state standards
- Explained that taste/odor issues may result from seasonal changes in groundwater
- Scheduled follow-up inspection for November 3, 1975
- Recommended flushing household pipes and cleaning faucet aerators

**Complainant Response:**
Mrs. Zona expressed dissatisfaction with previous responses and requested meeting with department supervisor. States multiple families in East Woburn area experiencing similar problems. Mentioned concerns about industrial activities near water sources. Requested written report of all testing conducted on Wells G and H since 1973.

**Internal Notes:**
This represents third complaint from Montvale Road area in past six weeks. Similar reports from:
- 94 Montvale Road (Anderson residence) - October 12, 1975
- 112 Montvale Road (Toomey residence) - October 20, 1975

Department receiving increased inquiries about Wells G and H water quality. Supervisor McKenna aware of pattern. May warrant additional testing beyond routine protocols.

**Disposition:**
- PENDING: Field inspection scheduled November 3, 1975
- PENDING: Supervisor review of complaint pattern
- PENDING: Consideration of expanded testing program

**Follow-up Required:**
- Contact complainant within 48 hours regarding supervisor meeting
- Coordinate with Health Department if additional complaints received
- Review operational status of Wells G and H filtration systems

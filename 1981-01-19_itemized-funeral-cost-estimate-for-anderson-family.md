## PAYMENT ARRANGEMENTS

We understand that funeral expenses can create financial hardship during an already difficult time. Woburn Memorial Funeral Home offers the following payment options:

- **Full payment upon services rendered**
- **50% deposit with balance due within 30 days**
- **Extended payment plan available upon request**

We also work directly with most life insurance companies and can assist with assignment of benefits to help ease the immediate financial burden on your family.

## ADDITIONAL CONSIDERATIONS

This estimate includes all standard services for a traditional funeral with burial. Should you wish to make any modifications to these arrangements, we are happy to adjust the estimate accordingly. Additional costs not included in this estimate might include:

- Flowers (if purchased through our florist)
- Special music or additional clergy
- Extended limousine service for additional family members
- Upgraded casket selection
- Special cemetery requirements beyond standard vault

Mrs. Anderson, please know that our staff is committed to honoring James's memory and supporting your family through this process. We have served many Woburn families over the years and understand the importance of both dignity and affordability in funeral services.

If you have any questions about this estimate or would like to discuss any modifications to the planned services, please do not hesitate to contact me directly. We can also provide references for grief counseling services and support groups that may be helpful for you and your other children during this time.
